    How to play

    Every day, everyone gets the same four numbers. Combine all four, using each exactly once, to make exactly 24.

    Tap a number, tap an operator, tap a second number. They merge into one tile. Keep going until one tile remains. If it reads 24, you've solved it.

    Example: 4, 7, 8, 8
    7 − 8 ÷ 8 = 6
    6 × 4 = 24

    Fractions along the way are shown exactly, never rounded. Undo steps back one merge, Reset starts the puzzle over.
